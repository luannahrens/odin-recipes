Prosciutto Wrapped Dates Stuffed with Goat Cheese

Easy, quick (and gluten-free friendly) recepie. This appitizer can be made ahead and chilled in the refriderator until ready to heat in oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of fresh dates
  • 4 oz package of sliced prosciutto
  • 5 oz goat cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Slice open dates and remove pit
  3. Spoon goat cheese into open date
  4. Slice prosciutto into strips, cut lengthwise and then in half
  5. Wrap cheese filled date with prosciutto
  6. Roast in oven until prosciutto is crispy
Omit cheese for dairy free version.
