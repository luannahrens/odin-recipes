Fish Tacos

In our house Fish Tacos are family favorite! They are easy and quick to make. This recipe uses unbreaded, broiled cod fish making this a healty choice for dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cod or other white fish
  • 2 T olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 1/2 t paprika
  • 2 t dried parsley
  • 1/2 t black pepper
  • 1/2 t salt
  • soft flour or corn tortillas√ü
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, shredded
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 large tomatoe, diced
  • Hidden Valley Southwest Chipotle Ranch Dressing
  • sour cream
  • 1 lime
  • 1 cup Kraft Mexican shredded cheese blend

Steps

  1. Preheat broiler to 500.
  2. Place fish in a 8 x 8 baking pan.
  3. In small bowl, mix together olive oil and garlic.
  4. Brush oil mixture onto fish.
  5. Season fish with parsley, paprika, black pepper, and salt
  6. Place seasoned fish under broiler for 5 minues.
  7. After 5 minutes, flip fish and brush again with oil mixture.
  8. Place fish back in broiler for another 5 minutes.
  9. Wrap torillas in paper towel, warm in microwave for 30 seconds.
  10. Break fish into pieces with a fork, and place in torilla.
  11. Use cabbage, carrots, cilantro, tomatoes, chipolte dressing, sour cream, and cheese as taco topping choices.
Enjoy!
