Stuffed Bell Peppersd

Stuffed bell peppers made healthy by including brown rice! This is a satifing meal.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz tomato sauce
  • 6 bell peppers, any color
  • 1 lb ground beef, 95% lean
  • 1 cup uncooked brown rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 1/2 t pepper
  • 1/2 t garlic power
  • 1 1/2 cup part-skim shredded mozarrella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cook brown rice
  3. De-seed bell peppers
  4. Place de-seeded peppers in sauce pan and cover peppers with water
  5. Bring water to boil, cook peppers for 10 minutes
  6. In fry pan, brown ground beef and drain
  7. Add chopped onion, spices, and tomato sauce to ground beef, simmer 10 minutes
  8. Mix in rice
  9. Place cooked peppers in 9 x 13 pan, stuff with beef & rice mixture
  10. Cook in oven for 45 minutes
  11. Top with cheese, cook additional 5 - 10 minutes (until cheese is melted)
Omit cheese for dairy free version.
